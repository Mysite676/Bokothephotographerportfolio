About Boko

Zambian Photographer with a Passion for Storytelling

Born and raised in Lusaka, Boko has been capturing life's special moments for over 4 years. With a deep passion for storytelling through the lens, Boko brings every photo and video to life with heart and detail

He specializes in wedding videography, advert shoots, and beautiful family portraits. Whether it's the joy of a wedding day, a powerful advert for your business, or a fun family shoot—Boko knows how to make every moment feel timeless.

Known for his friendly approach and creative eye, Boko works closely with clients to bring their vision to life. Every shot is more than just a picture—it's a memory captured forever.

Let Boko the Photographer turn your moments into lasting stories.
